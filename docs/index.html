Clear feedback, fast decisions.

Expose feedback is reviewed in a lightweight weekly loop, then converted into product decisions, private implementation work, or shipped release notes.

  1. 1New reports are labeled by type: question, idea, beta feedback, bug, private support, or security.
  2. 2Reproducible bugs and accepted ideas are moved into the private product roadmap.
  3. 3When a fix ships in TestFlight or the App Store, the public thread is updated.

Need a private route?

Email support when your report includes purchases, privacy requests, tokens, local URLs, personal screenshots, private files, or security details.
