# Code of Conduct

Expose is a small product community for practical help, thoughtful feedback, and clear bug reports.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and specific.
- Assume good intent, especially across languages and time zones.
- Keep reports focused on the product behavior you observed.
- Remove private data before posting screenshots, logs, routes, URLs, or configuration examples.
- Help other users when you know the answer.

## Not Allowed

- Harassment, hate speech, threats, or personal attacks.
- Posting someone else's private information.
- Sharing access tokens, private files, or sensitive local network details.
- Spam, unrelated promotion, or repeated duplicate posts.
- Publicly posting security vulnerabilities before maintainers have had a chance to respond.

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or delete content that breaks these expectations.
